Our approach

We want to talk to people in Central Lancashire who have experienced weight stigma, about their experiences with health services, and bring them together to share their experiences with each other. If you would like to share your experiences and connect with others, please email Stella at endweightstigma@stella-aster.com.

We are committed to being led by people in Central Lancashire with lived experiences of weight stigma. If you would like to be involved in our work, or if you have other ideas for how we can heal from, dismantle, and prevent weight stigma in our home, please email Stella at endweightstigma@stella-aster.com.
