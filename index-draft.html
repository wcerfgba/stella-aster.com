Stella Aster

I'm a social researcher and activist living in Leyland, Lancashire. I use my tech, research, and people skills to help local communities to understand and solve their problems.

What I'm doing

I'm a Patient Representative at Worden Medical Centre. I analyse patient feedback, take minutes in meetings, and help organise the patient newsletter.

I am a member of the Bookings Team at St Mary's Community Centre. I handle booking confirmations, help with tech issues, and am redesigning how we handle all of the information and enquiries.

I'm a PhD student, and my research aims to help higher weight patients and NHS staff in Blackpool to better meet their healthcare needs.

I am a volunteer at Radio Leyland. I built and maintain their What's On events listings page, and help out with tech issues.

I am forming the Leyland Trans Patient Support Group.

In the past I have been a member of Food Not Bombs Southampton. We accepted ingredients from local shops and cooked them into delicious vegan meals, which we served for free on the street as a demonstration against war.

Work with me

I have a wide range of tech and research skills. I can build websites, redesign processes and systems, carry out surveys, facilitate meetings and workshops, and conduct interviews. I'm also patient, kind, and a good listener and communicator.
